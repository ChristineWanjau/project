Models

These are our models

Our models include: Define, Deep dive, Design, Deliver.

Define

The definition part is the proposal stage where we define the issues in our terms to confirm the client is understood. We also include solutions and suggestions to the needs deduced during deep dive.

Deep Dive

This is the diagnosis stage. We engage and prod clients further as we seek to understand their needs.

Design

we develop “customized” scope of works and a list of activities hinged on the identified need.

Deliver

In this stage, we implement and measure results.
